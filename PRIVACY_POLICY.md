# Privacy Policy for Fillosophy

**Last updated:** July 18, 2026

Fillosophy ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the Fillosophy Chrome Extension ("Extension").

---

## 1. Information We Collect
Fillosophy processes data provided directly by you to enable resume parsing and form autofilling features:
- **Personal Information:** Name, email, phone number, location, work history, education history, and skills contained within your uploaded resume or profile.
- **User Content:** Uploaded resume files (PDF, DOCX) provided for text extraction.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information solely to:
- Extract relevant fields from your uploaded resume.
- Match extracted details with input fields on web application forms you choose to fill.
- Save your profile preferences locally within your browser.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Sharing
- **No Data Sales:** We do **not** sell, rent, trade, or monetize your personal data to any third party.
- **Backend Processing:** Resume files and form context are sent over encrypted HTTPS connections to our cloud backend on Render solely for processing via AI model APIs (Claude / Groq).
- **Local Storage:** Profile settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services
We utilize trusted cloud processing APIs (Render, Groq, Anthropic) strictly for processing form autofill requests. These services do not use your data for advertising or tracking.
